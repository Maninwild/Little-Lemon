About Us

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant nestled in the heart of Chicago, where tradition meets innovation. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Italy, Greece, and Turkey, our dishes celebrate authentic Mediterranean flavors with a modern twist. At Little Lemon, we believe in using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring that every bite bursts with flavor and quality. From hand-picked olives and sun-ripened tomatoes to aromatic herbs and premium olive oil, our menu is a reflection of our passion for genuine, wholesome cuisine. Our talented chefs, Mario and Adrian, bring decades of culinary expertise to your table, crafting time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're craving a classic Greek Salad, a hearty Italian Lasagna, or the smoky richness of a Turkish Kebab, every dish is prepared with meticulous attention to detail. More than just a restaurant, Little Lemon is a place where friends and family come together. Our cozy, welcoming atmosphere is perfect for intimate dinners, lively gatherings, or simply savoring the flavors of the Mediterranean with a glass of fine wine.
